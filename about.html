- Project Description

This Focus Story entitled “Garbo sa Lapu-Lapu” is a site that provides you information of all the bests in Lapu-Lapu City. The City of Lapu-Lapu (formerly called Opon) is a first class[1] highly urbanized city in the Philippines. It is geographically located in theprovince of Cebu, but administered independently from the province. It is part of the Cebu Metropolitan Area. The city showcases lots of attractive destinations which lured local and foreign tourists visit Lapu-Lapu City. Sugbuanons will come to the city of Lapu-Lapu to take a glimpse and experience the fun in city. Most of the local and foreign tourists enjoy picking up souvenirs such as shell crafts and quality guitars that is originally manufactured by the local people in Lapu-Lapu city.

This website will share to you some of the souvenirs that you can buy in the city. This site segments some of the souvenirs that you shouldn't miss when visiting here in Lapu-Lapu. This site also showcases the wonders and the creativity of each locals in Lapu-Lapu.

Naturally, people are fond of travelling and exploring to different places. Someone who journeys to a specific place or who uses a specific form of transportation is what we call a "traveler". Well then if you consider yourself one, come and visit Lapu-Lapu cit and experience its best.

- Methodology

The Group AHA together with the Team Alarm conducted a researched about Lapu-Lapu city. The group gathered some souvenir items particularly Shell crafts and guitars and learned about its composition. The group also collects information about the history and origin of the place.
